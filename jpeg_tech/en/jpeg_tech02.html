Significance

Since the amount data in a picture is large, an efficient method of compression is needed for transfer and storage. High speed compression is important, as are reduction in bandwidth, transfer time, and required memory.

Compatibility is an important criteria in the exchange of compressed data between various computer systems and picture handling systems.

JPEG is supported by many industries as an international standard for the coding of color still pictures.
